YourCallYourRule

Take complete control of your calls and SMS messages.

Stop Spam, Protect Your Privacy

YourCallYourRule is a powerful Android app that lets you customize your call and SMS blocking rules to fit your needs. Say goodbye to unwanted calls and messages!

Get it on Google Play Download from GitHub

Key Features

Customizable Blocking

Create rules based on phone numbers, keywords, and more.

Whitelisting & Blacklisting

Manage allowed and blocked numbers with ease.

Online Subscription

Subscribe to curated spam number lists (requires subscription URL).

Regular Expressions

Define complex blocking rules using regular expressions.

SIM Card Recognition

Identify incoming calls based on the SIM card used.

Local Data Management

All data is stored locally for maximum privacy.

Plugin Support

Extend functionality with custom plugins and online databases.

Backup & Restore

Support for WebDAV, Google Drive, and OneDrive for seamless data backup and restoration.

SMS Filtering (GitHub Version Only)

The GitHub version of YourCallYourRule offers powerful SMS filtering capabilities, allowing you to block unwanted SMS messages based on sender number or keywords.

Note: Due to Google Play policies, this feature is not available in the Play Store version. Choose the appropriate version from the GitHub releases.
